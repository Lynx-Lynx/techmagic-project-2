Queue of wishes for our 'neighbour dearest'

We all would love to write a few gentle words to a certain notorious someone.

Imagine that it is actually possible. What would you say?

Here we have a queue of short letters (maximum 50 characters) that are sent [possibly, by war geese] one at a time when the number of letters goes over 10.

Write anything to your liking into the input field and press the 'Enter' button / 'Enter' key.
